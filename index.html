About N.Flying

N.Flying is a South Korean idol band formed in 2013 by FNC Entertainment. The band's name, N.Flying, stands for "New Flying", symbolizing "new wings" or "new escape". They debuted in Japan on October 1, 2013, and in South Korea on May 20, 2015. Their fandom name is "N.Fia".
